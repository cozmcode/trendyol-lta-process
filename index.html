Responsibility Summary by Stakeholder

HRBP

  • Confirm employee profile & assignment terms
  • Set assignment duration & dates
  • Approve extension or repatriation
  • Respond to contract expiry alerts

Mobility Team

  • Issue offer & track signing
  • Assign vendors & mobility responsible
  • Conduct intro call & introduce vendors
  • Collect documents from assignee
  • Set up payroll & health insurance
  • Coordinate with all stakeholders

Immigration / Relocation Vendor

  • Document attestation (UAE: vendor; KSA: outsourced)
  • Entry permit application & tracking
  • Medical, biometrics, labour contract
  • Residency visa / Iqama application
  • Emirates ID / National ID tracking
  • Temporary & permanent accommodation
  • Visa renewal & extensions

Assignee

  • Sign offer letter
  • Provide required documents
  • Book flight after entry permit
  • Confirm arrival date
  • Open bank account (post Emirates ID)
  • Secure permanent housing (with vendor)

Automation Opportunities

What The Cozm can automate to eliminate manual tracking and missed deadlines

🔔 Slack / Email Reminders

Automated alerts for visa expiry, document deadlines, contract renewals, and housing setup — sent to the right person at the right time via Slack or email.

🔄 Status Auto-Progression

When a milestone is completed (e.g., entry permit issued), the next step is automatically triggered and the responsible party notified — no manual handover.

📈 Real-Time Dashboard

Replace the flat Google Sheet with a visual dashboard showing each assignee's progress, overdue items, and upcoming deadlines at a glance.

📧 Vendor Escalation

Auto-email immigration vendors (Fragomen, EER, Tamimi) when attestation, permits, or visas are delayed beyond threshold — with pre-drafted escalation templates.

📅 Calendar Integration

Key dates (assignment end, visa expiry, contract renewal) pushed directly into HRBP and Mobility Team calendars with advance reminders.

🤖 AI Query Responder

Auto-reply to common Slack/email questions like "What is Employee X's visa status?" or "When does Y's contract expire?" — pulling live data from the tracker.
